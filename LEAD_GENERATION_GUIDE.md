## üîÑ Method 2: Partner with Local Businesses

### Who to Partner With:

**Real Estate Agents**
- They work with people buying homes (need life insurance)
- Offer: Co-host a homebuying seminar
- They introduce you, you present life insurance
- Capture leads with QR code to your landing page

**Mortgage Brokers**
- Every homebuyer needs mortgage protection
- Offer: Pay them $50 per closed deal
- They add you to their process
- Automated lead flow

**Financial Advisors**
- They focus on investments, not insurance
- Offer: Handle their clients' insurance needs
- They refer clients to you
- You split commissions or pay referral fees

**Insurance Agents (Other Lines)**
- Auto insurance agents
- Home insurance agents
- They don't sell life, refer to you
- You pay them per lead or per sale

**Funeral Homes** (Morbid but effective)
- Pre-need funeral planning = final expense insurance
- Partner with funeral director
- They refer families planning ahead
- Very high conversion rate

## üë• Method 3: Referral System

### Automated Referral Requests:

1. **After placing a policy** (automated):
   ```
   Email/Text:
   "Hi [Name]! I'm so glad we got your policy approved.
   I'm looking to help more families like yours.
   If you know anyone who needs life insurance,
   I'll give you $50 for every referral that becomes a client.
   Just forward them this link: [Your unique referral link]"
   ```

2. **Client Referral Landing Page**:
   - Page that explains referral program
   - Simple form: "Who are you referring?"
   - Referrer gets tracked
   - Auto-payment when referral converts

3. **Incentive Structure**:
   - Current client: $50 per referral
   - Or: 10% of first-year commission
   - Or: Gift card (Amazon, restaurant)
   - Make it a no-brainer for them

Day 7: "The 5 biggest mistakes when buying life insurance"

- Response rate: 1-3%
